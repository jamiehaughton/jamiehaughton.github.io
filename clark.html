Clark Hall

Grace Scripps Clark Hall was the second residence hall at Scripps, completed in 1928.

According to Scripps' Website, "Clark was a joint gift of Mrs. Rex B. Clark (Grace Scripps Clark) and Miss Ellen Browning Scripps in memory of Mr. James E. Scripps, who was brother of the donors."

Clark is the western-most dorm on campus, just inside the wall bordering Colombia Avenue.
