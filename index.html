Scripps College is a member of the The Claremont Colleges, located in Claremont, CA, along with Pomona, Claremont McKenna, Harvey Mudd, and Pitzer Colleges.

Scripps has a total of nine residence halls, four of which are fondly referred to as the "old dorms" for their character and charm. The school also has three houses which are used as dorms for students.

The "not old" residence halls include: Frankel Hall, Routt Hall, GJW Hall, Kimberly/Wilbur Hall, and Schow Hall.
